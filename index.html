The Beginning - Scrubs

Thursday October 20, 2016

Amber and Adam met while working together as Resident Advisors at Algonquin College. They were assigned to work together in MacDonald House (AKA the Best House) and frequently worked together as duty partners.

After a few months of working together the two realized they are both rather wacky and silly and got along really well. They had discussed similar Netflix interests and were both interested in watching the show Scrubs. One night, Adam invited Amber up to his room to watch Scrubs together and the rest is history.

First Real Date - Putting Edge

Saturday November 5, 2016

After a few weeks of spending time together and getting to know each other, they had the opportunity to get to go on their first real date. They went to Hard Stones Grill for dinner, looked at some cute animals at PetSmart and then went glow in the dark mini-putting at Putting Edge.

The two played some arcade games including air hockey, skee ball, and free throw basketball, both winning a few games and playing together. However, Amber came out on top as the overall victor by winning the glow in the dark mini putt game.
